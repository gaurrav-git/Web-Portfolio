🎬 Cinematography & Reels Making

Cinematography is more than a hobby for me - it's how I tell stories! During my BBA at GGSIPU, I actively participated in cinematography and reels-making activities, and I'm proud to have secured 2nd position in both competitions at the annual college fest in 2023. I love the idea of capturing moments and crafting them into something visually compelling. The same eye for detail and storytelling that goes into filmmaking also helps me think about UI/UX and how users experience software.

3 Benefits of Cinematography:

  1. Sharpens creativity and visual storytelling - useful in designing great user interfaces.
  2. Teaches attention to detail, timing, and patience - all critical in software development.
  3. Builds confidence in presenting and communicating ideas clearly to others.
